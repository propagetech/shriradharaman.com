Braj Mandal Parikrama - Frequently Asked Questions

Braj 84 Kos Yatra

With Vaisnavacharya Shri Chandan Goswami

9th to 16th November 2025

General Information

1. What is included in the fee?
The fee covers 7 nights and 8 days of accommodation, meals (breakfast, lunch, dinner, and refreshments), guided visits to all major temples, and comfortable transportation in air-conditioned 4-wheeler cars.

2. What is the duration of the trip?
The trip lasts 7 nights and 8 days, during which we will visit the sacred pastime places of Lord Shri Krishn within 250 km of the Braj region.

3. Which destinations/temples will be covered on the trip?
A detailed itinerary will be shared closer to the yatra date.

4. Is the trip suitable for senior citizens and families?
Yes, the trip is thoughtfully designed for all age groups, including senior citizens and families, with comfortable arrangements throughout. While most locations are accessible by car, please note that some sites may require a short walk or climbing stairs.

Booking & Payment

5. What is the cost of the trip?
a. For individuals above 12 years of age: ₹ 25,000 per person*
b. For children between 4 and 12 years of age: ₹ 15,000 per person*
c. For children below 4 years of age: No payment required* *Costs are inclusive of all applicable government taxes.

6. How can I book and register for the trip?
You can register through our website .

7. What are the payment options?
We accept bank transfers, UPI, and cash.

8. What is the registration fee?
The registration fee is ₹ 5,000

9. By when should I make the remaining payment?
The remaining payment should be made immediately or by 20th September 2025 , at the latest.

10. Is the fee refundable in case of cancellation?
The registration fee of ₹ 5,000 is non-refundable (except for children under 4 years of age). The last date for cancellation is 30th September 2025 . After this date, no refunds will be issued.

11. Can I transfer my booking to someone else if I’m unable to go?
Yes, bookings can be transferred at least 7 days before the trip starts.

12. What documents do I need to carry?
You must carry a valid government-issued ID (Aadhaar, Voter ID, or Passport) and a printed or digital copy of your registration confirmation.

13. Can I register on behalf of someone else?
Yes, you can register for family or friends, but their details must be submitted at the time of registration.

14. Can I make a group booking?
Yes, group bookings are available. Payments can be made for any number of persons, however, registration forms still needs to be filled per individual.

15. Do I need a physical ticket or is a con fi rmation email enough?
A digital copy of your booking confirmation is required at check-in.

16. Will I receive a registration con fi rmation?
Yes, a confirmation email will be sent upon successful payment.

17. What is the last date for payment?
The last date to register is 20th September 2025 .

Payment Options

18. Can I pay in cash?
We encourage digital payments.

19. Are there any additional taxes or hidden charges?
No, the price is all-inclusive.

Cancellations & Refunds

20. What is the cancellation policy?

Cancellations made before 30th September 2025 are eligible for a partial refund:

a. Above 12 years: ₹ 20,000 per person

b. Children 4–12 years: ₹ 10,000 per person No refunds for last-minute cancellations.

21. How do I cancel my booking?
Please contact customer support +918859603313

22. Will I get a full refund if I cancel?
No. A cancellation fee of ₹ 5,000 will be deducted for participants aged 4 and above. A full refund of ₹ 5,000 is available for children under 4 years.

23. What if I need to leave the trip early?
No refunds will be provided for early departures.

Accommodation & Meals

24. What type of accommodation will be provided?
We offer clean, comfortable, and peaceful accommodations suitable for spiritual travelers. Depending on the location, stays may be arranged in hotels, guesthouses, or ashram-style lodgings. Rooms are typically on a twin-sharing basis unless specified otherwise.

25. What kind of meals will be provided?
We serve pure vegetarian satvik prasadi meals — breakfast, lunch, and dinner.

Travel & Logistics

26. What mode of transport will be used?
Air-conditioned 4-wheeler cars.

27. From where does the Yatra start and end each day?
Each day will begin and end at your accommodation in Vrindavan.

28. Will there be a tour guide during the Yatra?
Yes, a knowledgeable Panda (traditional guide) will accompany us throughout, sharing insights into the sacred places, their history, and spiritual significance.

Other Considerations

29. Will medical assistance be available in case of emergency?
Yes, basic medical support will be available, and emergency help will be arranged if needed.

30. Can I bring my children?
Yes, children are welcome and must be registered.

31. Is photography allowed in temples?
Photography rules vary by temple. Please follow each temple’s guidelines.

32. What is the appropriate dress code?
Wear modest, traditional Indian clothing covering sleeves and ankles.

a. Women: Saree, salwar kameez, long skirt with top, or pants with kurti

b. Men: Kurta with dhoti, pajama, or pants Carry winter clothing as required.

33. Will there be free time for personal exploration?
Yes, some free time will be available in the evenings after returning to the accommodation.

Trip Details & Customization

34. Can I extend my stay beyond the 8-day trip?
The official trip duration is 8 days. Any extended stay must be arranged independently.

35. Will there be sankirtan or bhajans during the trip?
Yes, devotional sessions including sankirtans and Kathas by Maharaj Ji will be held daily at selected locations.

36. Can I skip certain temple visits if I need rest?
Yes, you may rest at your convenience. However, the group itinerary will proceed as planned.

Health & Safety

37. Is the trip physically demanding?
Some temples may require walking or climbing stairs.

38. Do I need any vaccinations or medical check-ups?
No specific vaccinations are required. However, carry your prescribed medicines.

39. Will fi rst-aid facilities be available?
Yes, basic medical support will be provided.

Group & Solo Travelers

40. Will the group be mixed-gender, or are there separate arrangements?
The group will be mixed. However, shared accommodations can be arranged based on gender upon request.

Temple Visits & Spiritual Activities

41. Are special puja services included?
General darshans are included. Special pujas/sevas can be arranged at an additional cost as per temple guidelines.

42. Do I need to follow speci fi c customs at temples?
Yes, we will guide you on proper temple etiquette.

Food & Drinks

43. Will drinking water be provided?
Yes, packaged drinking water will be available.

44. Can I bring my own snacks/beverages?
Yes, as long as they are vegetarian and non-alcoholic.

45. Will tea/co ff ee be available?
Yes, at designated meal times.

46. Will special food be available for children or elderly participants with dietary needs?
We serve satvik prasadi , but can accommodate specific dietary requests due to medical needs with prior notice.

Luggage & Personal Items

47. Are laundry facilities available?
Laundry services may be available at your accommodation, at your own cost.

48. Can I bring my own pillow/blanket?
Yes, if preferred for comfort.

49. Will charging facilities be available?
Yes, you can charge mobile phones and cameras at the accommodations. Power banks are recommended.

Communication & Support

50. Will mobile connectivity be available?
Some locations may have limited network access.

51. Who do I contact during the trip in case of an issue?
A dedicated helpline number will be provided.

52. Will there be a WhatsApp group for updates?
Yes, all participants will be added to a WhatsApp group closer to the travel date.
