Radharaman Gita-Nitya Raag Seva

A collection of 35 Brajbhasha songs that have serenaded Shri Radharamanji for over a century, Radharaman Gita-Nitya Raag Seva lovingly portrays Shri Radharaman’s daily life ( Nitya Lila ) from morning to night. These beautiful tracks are sung in the traditional Haveli Sangeet style, accompanied by instruments such as the pakhawaj , sitar , jaanjh and sanrangi .
